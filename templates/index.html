About Us


Highland Properties is a 25-year-old real estate firm headquartered in Kerala. Our goal is to provide our customers with the highest quality commercial real estate services available, while maintaining a focus on customer service and satisfaction. We aim to be your go-to source for all of your commercial real estate needs. Whether you're looking for offices or warehouses, Highland will find the perfect space for your company's needs. We offer our clients a wide variety of options in each category, but if nothing suits your needs we'll work with you to develop an innovative solution that meets your specifications. We believe in providing honest advice and top-quality service so that our clients can make informed decisions about their investments and grow their businesses without fear of being left behind by the competition. At Highland Properties, we understand that commercial real estate is not just about buying and selling property. It's also about finding the right tenants for your property, and having a team of professionals who can deliver on your vision for the space.

Developers We Deal


At Highland Properties, we collaborate with some of Kerala’s most reputable and trusted real estate developers to offer premium commercial and residential spaces. Our network includes developers renowned for their quality, innovation, and commitment to sustainability. From luxury apartments and villas to state-of-the-art office spaces and industrial warehouses, we ensure that every project meets the highest standards in construction and design. Our partnerships with these developers allow us to offer properties in prime locations across Kerala, including major cities like Kochi, Trivandrum, and Calicut, as well as emerging commercial hubs. We work closely with developers who share our vision of delivering properties that not only meet current market demands but also anticipate future growth. Whether it's contemporary office buildings or environmentally friendly residential communities, our developers integrate cutting-edge technologies and sustainable practices to create spaces that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing. By dealing with Highland Properties, you gain access to a broad range of real estate options, from affordable office spaces to premium business hubs, ensuring that your investment yields both short-term and long-term benefits. Trust us to connect you with the best developers who can help bring your real estate vision to life.
